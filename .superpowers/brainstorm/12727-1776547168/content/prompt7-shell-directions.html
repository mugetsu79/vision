Hybrid Command Center

Balanced between product polish and operator usefulness. Good if you want CRUD pages to feel premium without losing the control-room character.

My recommendation

  • C is the strongest default for Argus.
  • It gives us a polished app shell for Prompt 7 and a natural bridge into Prompt 8’s live telemetry dashboard.

Watch-out

  • A can feel too infrastructure-heavy for routine CRUD flows.
  • B risks feeling too generic unless we deliberately preserve Argus’s monitoring identity.
