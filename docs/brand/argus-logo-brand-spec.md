Date: 2026-04-19

## Goal

Create a final primary logo for Argus that feels:

- premium
- modern
- vigilant
- connected
- trustworthy
- future-ready
- native to dark-mode enterprise software

The logo must work across product UI, operator dashboards, hardware labeling, investor materials, web marketing, and documentation.

## Alignment With The Brand Intent

The chosen direction already aligns well with the intended Argus identity in the following ways:

- strong eye plus network/radar symbolism
- cerulean intelligence core and violet perimeter shift
- dark, high-contrast, premium presentation
- strong prominence of `Argus`
- modern technology tone appropriate for a vision intelligence platform

The chosen direction is good enough to refine, rather than restart from zero.

## Final Revision Brief

Use **Image 2** as the master direction. Keep **Image 1** only as a reference for slightly stronger glow energy, not as the base.

### Brand Goal

Create the final primary logo for **Argus | The OmniSight Platform**: a premium, modern B2B identity that communicates vigilant intelligence, universal visibility, and advanced connected technology. The mark should feel trustworthy, future-ready, and strong in dark-mode product UI, hardware labeling, investor decks, and marketing.

### What To Keep

- The core concept: abstract eye plus radar or network hub.
- The cerulean center with violet perimeter transition.
- The dark obsidian presentation.
- The strong hierarchy of `Argus` first, platform descriptor second.
- The overall premium, luminous, high-tech tone.

### What To Improve

- Simplify the icon slightly so it reads faster and scales better at small sizes.
- Reduce visual busyness in the intersecting shards.
- Make the glow more restrained and controlled, especially around the outer perimeter.
- Reduce the “render/mockup” feel from bevels and shadows.
- Remove decorative artifacts such as the small sparkle in the corner.
- Make the tagline clearly secondary to `Argus`.
- Increase distinctiveness in the typography so it feels less generic.

### Icon Direction

- Preserve the geometric eye structure and central intelligence node.
- Keep the feeling of motion and omnidirectional awareness.
- Sharpen the silhouette so the eye reads instantly even at favicon or app-icon scale.
- Use fewer internal overlaps if possible.
- Maintain precise edges, but soften them just enough to feel refined rather than aggressive.
- The icon should suggest “watchful intelligence” first and “networked platform” second.

### Color And Lighting

- Background: deep obsidian black with subtle charcoal gradient.
- Core glow: vivid cerulean blue.
- Outer shift: ethereal violet.
- Light should feel like controlled digital bioluminescence, not neon signage.
- Avoid oversaturation and avoid a gaming aesthetic.
- Off-white text should harmonize with the brightest highlights of the icon.

### Typography

- `Argus` should remain bold, luminous, and dominant.
- The separator `|` should stay subtle and elegant.
- `THE OMNISIGHT PLATFORM` should be lighter weight and slightly smaller.
- Tighten the brand system so the type feels more signature and less stock.
- Favor a refined geometric sans direction, not something overly rounded or playful.

### Composition

- Keep the icon centered above the wordmark lockup.
- Maintain strong balance between symbol and wordmark.
- Ensure the logo works both centered and horizontal if needed later.
- Prioritize clarity on dark UI surfaces.

### Required Deliverables

- Primary hero version with restrained glow.
- Clean product or UI version with minimal effects.
- Monochrome version for print, embossing, hardware, and low-ink contexts.
- App icon or square mark version.
- Small-scale test version to confirm legibility.

### Do Not

- Do not make it flatter and more generic.
- Do not increase complexity in the icon.
- Do not push the violet glow further than Image 1.
- Do not let the tagline compete with `Argus`.
- Do not keep obvious AI-generation ornamentation or presentation artifacts.

### Success Test

The final logo should feel like:

- premium enterprise software
- vigilant intelligence
- modern dark-mode control platform
- scalable across UI, hardware, and brand surfaces

### One-Line Creative Direction

Refine Image 2 into a cleaner, more scalable, premium enterprise master logo with a simplified eye-network icon, restrained cerulean-to-violet glow, stronger typography hierarchy, and less mockup-style rendering.

The icon should feel geometric, precise, modern, and intelligent, with a clean central cerulean blue glow that softly transitions into ethereal violet at the outer edges. The lighting should feel like controlled digital bioluminescence, not neon signage.

```text
Use Image 2 as the visual direction: balanced composition, restrained glow, premium dark-mode presentation, strong brand clarity. Simplify the icon slightly so it scales better and reads faster. Reduce internal clutter and excessive overlap. Keep the eye silhouette immediately recognizable. The symbol should suggest vigilance first and connected intelligence second.

Typography: “Argus” large, bold, luminous off-white, elegant geometric sans-serif, premium and modern. Add a subtle vertical separator “|”. Place “THE OMNISIGHT PLATFORM” as a secondary line or secondary block in smaller, lighter-weight off-white text. The tagline must be clearly subordinate to “Argus”.

Style: sophisticated B2B platform identity, future-ready, dark-mode native, premium, confident, elegant, restrained, technical, trustworthy. Avoid consumer/gaming vibes. Avoid cheesy neon, excessive glow, clutter, or ornamental AI artifacts. No sparkle icons, no extra symbols, no busy textures. The final result should look like a real master brand mark for a high-end vision intelligence platform.

Deliver a centered hero lockup with icon above wordmark, polished but clean, suitable for product UI, website hero, pitch deck, and hardware branding.
```

## SVG / Vector Art-Direction Brief

Use this for the vector execution pass:

- Build the icon as a clean geometric mark, not a painted effect.
- Base form: almond-shaped eye silhouette inside a circular radar or network frame.
- Center: one circular “intelligence core” node.
- Internal structure: 3 to 4 interlocking directional blades or shards maximum.
- The eye outline must remain readable at small size without glow.
- Reduce thin protrusions that may disappear in favicon or app-icon scale.
- Prioritize silhouette clarity over internal complexity.
- Create these layers separately:
  - base vector icon
  - gradient fills
  - optional glow layer
  - wordmark
  - tagline
- Gradient direction:
  - inner core: cerulean `#35B8FF`
  - midtones: electric sky `#5BC8FF`
  - outer edge: violet `#9B7CFF`
- Text color: luminous off-white `#F4F7FB`
- Background reference: obsidian `#0A0D12` to charcoal `#151A22`
- Effects should be optional overlays, not baked into the core mark.
- Export variants:
  - full-color glow lockup
  - flat or digital lockup
  - one-color light
  - one-color dark
  - symbol-only
  - square app-icon crop
- Spacing rules:
  - keep clear space around logo equal to at least the width of the icon core
  - tagline width should not visually exceed the width of `Argus`
- Typography direction:
  - bold geometric sans for `Argus`
  - lighter matching sans for tagline
  - subtle separator
  - no condensed techno font
  - no playful rounded font

## Brand Board

### Name

- `Argus`
- descriptor: `The OmniSight Platform`

### Brand Attributes

- vigilant
- intelligent
- connected
- premium
- dark-mode native
- enterprise-ready

### Color Palette

- `Obsidian`: `#0A0D12`
- `Charcoal`: `#151A22`
- `Cerulean Core`: `#35B8FF`
- `Sky Glow`: `#5BC8FF`
- `Violet Edge`: `#9B7CFF`
- `Soft Lilac Highlight`: `#C7B8FF`
- `Luminous White`: `#F4F7FB`
- `Muted Steel`: `#94A0B2`

### Typography

- wordmark direction: `Sora Bold` or `Space Grotesk Bold`
- product and UI type: `Inter`, `Manrope`, or `Plus Jakarta Sans`
- use `Argus` in bold, descriptor in medium or semibold
- all-caps descriptor is acceptable if tracking is slightly open

### Logo Usage

- Preferred on dark backgrounds.
- Glow version for hero and marketing contexts.
- Flat version for app chrome and documentation.
- Monochrome version for hardware, print, embossing, and favicon constraints.

### Visual Language

- subtle luminescence
- controlled depth
- matte-screen feel
- geometric precision
- no decorative sparkles or unnecessary ornaments

### What Good Looks Like

- Feels like a serious AI and video intelligence platform.
- Reads clearly at both hero size and small UI size.
- Distinctive without being loud.
- Premium rather than flashy.
